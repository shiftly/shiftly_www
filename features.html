More Than Just a Roster

It’s everything you need to manage, message and coordinate your staff!

Super easy to use

Say goodbye to the old days. Shiftly’s intuitive drag-n-drop interface makes light work of what used to be a painful and time consuming task. Create shifts, assign staff and edit your roster from week to week with the click of a button.

Setup in minutes, not hours

Shiftly’s intuitive workflow has been designed to save you time. You can setup your first roster within minutes. Once setup, the weekly task of assigning your staff shifts becomes a sinch, allowing you to send out your roster with minimal effort. And with Facebook login, your staff can get going in seconds too.

Access Shiftly from anywhere, anytime

With Shiftly, your roster lives securely online. This means that you can update your roster from anywhere - you don’t have to wait for downtime in the backroom to grab the ruler and pencil anymore. Staff can also check their roster out of hours, and from any computer or mobile device. No more excuses for forgotten shifts!

Stay on top of your budget

Shiftly allows you to keep an eye on your budget. You can easily view how much you are spending per shift and per week. Just set the pay rates of your staff and your weekly budget and Shiftly will do the rest.

Manage staff and multiple admins

New staff - no worries! It’s easy to add and remove staff from your roster. Simply enter in their details and your staff members will automatically be sent an invite to register. You can also have multiple admins so that more than one user can edit and change your roster.

Customise your roster with groups

Groups allow you to easily colour and arrange shifts.Use and create groups to suit your businesses needs. You can assign different colours to differentiate multiple rosters, or colour code shifts by the time of the day. It’s your roster, your way!

Keep track of staff availability

Your staff can enter in their availability, and Shiftly will kindly let you know if you are rostering someone on who has said they are unavailable. If Timmy has Uni on Tuesday’s, Shiftly will let you know!

We'll notify your staff for you

Saving you the hassle, staff are automatically notified when a new roster is created or when one of their shifts are updated. Each user can choose to be notified by email, SMS or both. Of course they can still check their roster anytime by logging in.

Send one message not dozens

Easily message your staff from within Shiftly. We already have their contact details, so we allow you to message individual staff members or send shout out to everyone. Send that staff meeting reminder with one click.

Get more out of your data

Effortlessly view reports and statistics. Compare your budget week to week, check out your yearly spend and many more valuable interpretations of your data. Coming soon - Advanced Reporting!
