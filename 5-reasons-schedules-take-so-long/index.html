1. You’re using a spreadsheet, or still using paper rosters!

In 1963, you could get away with doing rosters on paper.
In 1983, people probably did schedules on whiteboards.
In 1993, you may have even moved to spreadsheets on your fancy new PC.
Let face it, it’s 2013 and there’s a better way of rostering your staff.

It’s called Shiftly, and it’s an awesome online scheduling app for your business.

2. You either call every staff member to tell them their shifts, or hope they’ve seen the roster posted at work.

With Shiftly, you schedule your shifts and then click publish. Every scheduled staff member then get’s a custom email with a list of their shifts.

3. You forget about someones availability and have to reschedule.

Shiftly manages staff availability so that you’ll automatically be warned if you’re scheduling a staff member for a shift they can’t attend.

4. You worry that you’ve missed a key role in your schedule.

If you run a cafe and only schedule 1 person in the kitchen instead of 2, you’re going to have a lot of problems. Shiftly allows you to split your business into groups that help you delegate staff and highlight where you’ve missed any assignments.

5. You want to give your staff an equal amount of shifts.

Do you worry that you keep putting on the same team and leaving some staff short-changed? Wouldn’t it be nice to filter your roster by each staff member and see a visual calendar of all their shifts?

Shiftly does it, and it’s awesome.
