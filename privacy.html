200 Okay! uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are required to use the Shiftly service.

We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your Shiftly account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

200 Okay! may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

Changes

200 Okay! may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Shiftly primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
